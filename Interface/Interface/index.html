In the future, with rapid technological advancements, robots have become an indispensable part of human life.

As an engineer responsible for developing intelligent AI to provide more convenient services to human society, you find that as robots gradually integrate into human life, some robots begin to exhibit perplexing behavior.

You receive a task to investigate the abnormal behavior of a group of robots. During the investigation, you discover that these robots surprisingly exhibit emotions and intentions similar to those of humans.

You can choose:

  • Accept and promote the development of robots.
  • Strengthen regulations to protect humanity.
  • Sever the intelligent connection of the robots.
